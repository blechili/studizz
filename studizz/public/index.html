Study Module
📄

Document Upload

Drag-and-drop PDFs, JPG scans, or PNG images up to 20 MB. Studizz detects digital text and uses OCR for scanned pages.

🧠

AI Summarisation

GPT-4o-mini produces a 3–5 paragraph academic overview that captures the core argument and key conclusions of any document.

🔑

Key Points

Six to ten concise bullet points pulled directly from the summary — perfect for last-minute revision the night before an exam.

📝

Interactive Quiz

Ten MCQs generated from your document. Select answers, submit, and receive instant grading with explanations for every question.

🏆

Score Metrics

An animated score ring shows your percentage. Below it, every question is reviewed with your answer, the correct answer, and a rationale.

▶️

YouTube Recommendations

Studizz queries YouTube for the four most relevant educational videos on your document's topic and stores them permanently with your summary.

Store Module
🗂

Summaries Archive

Every generated summary is automatically archived with file name, creation date, and a preview — searchable and accessible anytime.

📁

Custom Folders

Create colour-coded folders (Biology, History, Maths…) and organise summaries into them. Full create, rename, recolour, and delete support.

🗃

Folder-Wide Quiz

Select any folder and generate a single comprehensive 15-question quiz that spans every summary inside — ideal for cumulative exam prep.

AI Chatbot
🤖

Studizz AI Chatbot

A persistent, multi-turn conversational assistant that answers study questions, explains concepts, and guides you through the platform.

🔢

Daily Query Counter

A live counter shows exactly how many of your 15 daily queries remain. The input locks automatically when the limit is reached.

🔒

Private by Design

Zero API keys on the client. All AI calls route through a secure PHP backend. Your profile is stored only in the server database, never in your browser.
